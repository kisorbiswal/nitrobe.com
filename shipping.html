Shipping and Delivery

Last updated: Nov 29, 2024

At Nitrobe, we strive to ensure that your orders are shipped and delivered on time through trusted courier and postal services.

1. Shipping Methods

  • Orders are shipped via registered domestic couriers or speed post.
  • For international customers, shipping is managed through trusted international courier services or speed post.

2. Delivery Timelines

  • Orders are typically shipped within 0-7 days or as per the delivery date agreed upon during order confirmation.
  • Delivery times may vary based on courier or postal service norms. Delays caused by external factors are beyond our control.

3. Delivery Address

  • Orders will be delivered to the address provided during checkout. Please ensure the address is accurate and complete.
  • We are not responsible for delivery failures caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses provided by the customer.

4. Confirmation of Delivery

Once your order is shipped, delivery confirmation will be sent to the email address registered during the purchase. Please check your email for updates.
