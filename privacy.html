Privacy Policy

Last updated: Nov 29, 2024

Nitrobe ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Your name, contact details, and email address.
  • Demographic information such as postal code, preferences, and interests.
  • Information related to customer surveys, feedback, or offers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect your information to provide better services and for the following purposes:

  • To process transactions and manage your orders.
  • To improve our products, services, and website experience.
  • To send promotional emails about new products, offers, or updates.
  • To conduct market research and contact you via email or phone, where applicable.
  • To personalize your website experience based on your preferences.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience. We use cookies to:

  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Personalize your experience based on your preferences.
  • Identify which pages are most visited to enhance website functionality.

Cookies do not grant us access to your device or personal information, other than the data you choose to share. You can accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. However, declining cookies may limit your website experience.

4. Controlling Your Personal Information

You may control how your personal information is used in the following ways:

  • Opt-out of direct marketing by checking the appropriate box when filling forms on the website.
  • If you previously agreed to marketing communications, you can opt out anytime by contacting us at kisor@nitrobe.com.

We will not sell, distribute, or lease your personal information to third parties unless required by law or with your explicit permission.

5. Security of Your Information

We are committed to securing your personal information. We have implemented appropriate measures to prevent unauthorized access, data breaches, or disclosure.

6. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We may share your information with trusted third parties, such as payment gateways, to process transactions. We do not share your information for any purpose other than to fulfill your orders or comply with legal requirements.

7. Updating or Correcting Your Information

If you believe that any information we hold about you is incorrect or incomplete, please contact us at kisor@nitrobe.com. We will promptly update or correct your details.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at:

Email: kisor@nitrobe.com
