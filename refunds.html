At Nitrobe, we aim to deliver a seamless shopping experience. Below are the terms governing cancellations, refunds, and returns:

1. Refund Policy

  • Refunds will be credited to the customer's account within 3-5 business days.
  • Refunds will only be processed for valid requests as per our policy.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be canceled before they are shipped. To cancel, contact our support team.
  • Once shipped, orders cannot be canceled.

3. Return Policy

Food Items

  • Food items cannot be returned unless they are defective or damaged upon delivery.
  • If the item is damaged or defective, please contact support for assistance with a replacement or refund. Replacement will be done within 3-5 business days.
