Cancellations and Refunds

Last updated: Nov 29, 2024

At Nitrobe, we aim to deliver a seamless shopping experience. Below are the terms governing cancellations, refunds, and returns:

1. Refund Policy

  • Refunds will be credited to the customer's account within 3-5 business days after approval.
  • Refunds will only be processed for valid requests as per our policy.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be canceled before they are shipped. To cancel, contact our support team.
  • Once shipped, orders cannot be canceled.

3. Return Policy

Food Items

  • Food items cannot be returned unless they are defective or damaged upon delivery.
  • If the item is damaged or defective, please contact support for assistance with a replacement or refund.

Non-Food Items

  • Non-food items can be returned within 14 days of delivery.
  • All returned items must be unused, in their original packaging, and include proof of purchase.
  • Return shipping charges may apply unless the item is defective or incorrect.
